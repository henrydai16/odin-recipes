Taco bell's beefy crunch burrito.

Mmm... look at this delicious beefy crunch burrito. After five years, it's finally made it's god damn return. Inside is soft rice, crunchy fire chips, tasty seasoned beef, and gooey nacho cheese.

    You will need:

  • Many 8-12 inch tortillas
  • Mexican rice
  • Hot Fritos chips
  • Beef and taco bell seasoned packets
  • Nacho cheese

Here are the steps to make it!

  1. Cook the beef!
    • Make sure to use the taco bell seasoning packet!
  2. Cook the mexican rice
  3. Heat the tortilla up...
  4. Heat the nacho cheese up!

    5. Now we assemble!

  5. Place the rice in the tortilla, the chips, the beef, and the nacho cheese
  6. Make sure you wrap it nice and tight!
