Mi-Goreng!

You have to try this instant noodle classic, Mie-goreng! It is amazing and absolutely delicious!

Ingredients needed:

  • Mie-goreng noodles!
    • Yes it must be the iconic Mie-goreng brand.
  • Eggs
  • Water!

Let's get started!

  1. First you're going to want to boil the water.
  2. Once the water is boiled, place the noodles in (I use two packets)
  3. Cook the noodles to your liking, then use a strainer and get the noodles to be dry, place in a bowl.
    • Some people like their noodles chewy! It's all up to preference.
  4. Put in the seasoning packets, how much you put in is up to you.
  5. Fry up an egg, place it on top of the noodles, and voila!
